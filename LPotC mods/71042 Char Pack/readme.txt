The following languages use official translations from the set's instruction manual:
English, American, French, German, Spanish, French Canadian, LA Spanish

The Brazilian translation is official, but may or may not be in European Portuguese.

All other translations are unofficial and machine assisted. Ditto for "Filigree Vest".
In such translations, "Masthead" has been translated as "Statue", for safety.
Any input and/or constructive feedback from native speakers is greatly appreciated.
(especially for how to translate "masthead", being the statue at the front of a ship)

Specifically, constructive feedback is appreciated for the following translations:
Italian, Danish, Russian, Czech, Polish, Swedish, Norwegian, Brazilian, Dutch
